Grading scale
  • 90-100%: very good (5)
  • 80-89%: good plus (4.5)
  • 70-79%: good (4)
  • 60-69%: satisfactory plus (3.5)
  • 50-59%: satisfactory (3)
  • 0-49%: unsatisfactory (2)
Passing conditions
  • attendance: 20%
  • homework: 20%
  • midterm examination: 30%
  • final examination: 30%
  • bonus for attendance: 2%
  • bonus for homework: 2%
  • bonus for promoting Korean culture: ?

Two unwarranted absences do not affect the final score. Each subsequent absence results in the attendance score being reduced by 10%, i.e. from 20% to 10%, and from 10% to 0%. In order to pass the course, the student must attend at least 9 lectures and take the midterm and final examinations.

Homework
  1. Do the homework given after each class.
  2. Upload a copy to MS Teams no later than 11:59 PM on Wednesday in the following week.
