Grading scale
  • 90-100%: very good (5)
  • 80-89%: good plus (4.5)
  • 70-79%: good (4)
  • 60-69%: satisfactory plus (3.5)
  • 50-59%: satisfactory (3)
  • 0-49%: unsatisfactory (2)
Passing conditions
  • attendance: 20%
  • tests: 40%
  • presentation: 40%
  • bonus for attendance: 2%
  • bonus for promoting Korean culture: ?

Two unwarranted absences do not affect the final score. Each subsequent absence results in the attendance score being reduced by 10%, i.e. from 20% to 10%, and from 10% to 0%. In order to pass the course, the student must attend at least 9 lectures, take two tests, and prepare and deliver a presentation.

Presentation
  1. Each student is required to prepare and deliver an 18-minute presentation at a scheduled date.
  2. The topic of the presentation should be connected to the Confucianism-related subjects taught in the classroom.
  3. The presentation consists of 15 minutes of speech and 3 minutes of discussion.
  4. The student must upload all presentation materials to MS Teams by 7 PM on the day before their presentation.
