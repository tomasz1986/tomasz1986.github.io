1. Basic thesis preparation and writing process

  1. Choose a specific field of your interest.
  2. In the field, choose a subject to research.
  3. Search for and analyse existing studies.
  4. Come up with a novel topic on the subject.
  5. Verify subject-related material availability.
  6. Choose appropriate approach and methods.
  7. Ask for and receive your supervisors' approval.
  8. Searching for relevant and useful research materials.
  9. Explain the necessity and main goal of your research.
  10. Summarise existing studies and theoretical background.
  11. On your own, conduct the main part of your research.
  12. Conclude the study with a summary and an abstract.
  13. Check and verify formatting of the whole text.
  14. Ask your supervisors and other students for comments.
  15. Submit the final version of your thesis to the university.
