Passing conditions
  • attendance: 30 pts
  • quizzes: 20 pts
  • presentation: 50 pts

Each unwarranted absence reduces the attendance score by 2 points. In order to pass the course, a student must attend at least 10 lectures, take both quizzes, and deliver a presentation.

Grading scale
  • 90-100%: very good (5)
  • 80-89%: good plus (4.5)
  • 70-79%: good (4)
  • 60-69%: satisfactory plus (3.5)
  • 50-59%: satisfactory (3)
  • 0-49%: unsatisfactory (2)
Quizzes
  1. Each student is required to take two scheduled quizzes during the semester.
  2. The first quiz covers the material taught since the beginning of the semester.
  3. The second quiz covers the material taught after the first quiz.
  4. Each quiz consists of 10 questions and takes approximately 20 minutes.
Presentation
  1. Student in pairs are required to prepare and deliver a 20-minute presentation at the end of the semester.
  2. The topic of the presentation should be connected to the Korea-related subjects taught in the classroom.
  3. The students must submit all materials (PowerPoint, script, etc.) to the lecturers by 7 PM on the day before the presentation.
