Homework

Homework 1

  1. At least 5 times during the semester, finish translating the text after the class.
  2. Firstly, directly after each Korean sentence, write its English translation.
  3. Secondly, if necessary, fix the translation, so that the sentences connect naturally.
  4. Upload the finished translation to its corresponding task on MS Teams by midnight on Sunday in the same week.

Homework 2

  1. Choose a subject, which you are interested in, and then propose it during the class on March 5.
  2. On MS Teams, you will be assigned a task with a newspaper article on the previously selected topic.
  3. Divide the article into 3-4 sentence paragraphs, and then, if possible, divide each sentence into shorter fragments as long as each of them makes sense.
  4. Before each paragraph, prepare a list of new or difficult vocabulary and grammatical structures.
  5. Translate the whole text in the same manner as texts translated during the class.
  6. Upload the finished translation to its corresponding task on MS Teams by midnight on Friday in a week that precedes your homework presentation date.
  7. Guide translation of a selected text fragment together with the group on a previously scheduled date.
