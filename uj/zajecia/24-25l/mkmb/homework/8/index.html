Modern Korean in Media and Business (3)

Julia Zawierucha - plastic surgery for foreigners in Korea

  • 성형 - plastic surgery
  • 소비 - spending (money)
  • 조(兆) - trillion
  • 돌파하다 - to surpass, to break through

  • 펴다 - to smooth out/straighten

번역

0-1. 미용·성형 소비 첫 1兆 돌파...

Beauty procedures and plastic surgery spending exceeds 1 trillion won for the first time…

0-2. "여행와서 주름 펴고 쌍꺼풀 수술"

“Came on a trip and got wrinkle treatment and double eyelid surgery”

어휘

  • 한류 - Korean Wave (Hallyu)
  • 방한하다 - to visit Korea
  • 관련(된) - related
  • 소비액 - amount of spending, expenditure
  • 넘어서다 - to surpass

번역

1. 한류와 K-뷰티의 인기가 높아지고 있다.

The popularity of Hallyu and K-beauty is rising.

2. 지난해 방한한 외국인의 미용·성형 관련 소비액이 처음으로 1조원을 넘어섰다.

Last year, for the first time, the spending by foreigners visiting Korea on beauty and cosmetic surgery-related services exceeded 1 trillion won.

어휘

  • 의료관광 - medical tourism
  • 비중 - proportion, share
  • 차지하다 - to account for, to occupy

  • 피부과 - dermatology
  • 성형외과 - plastic surgery
  • 크게 앞서다 - to be far ahead

번역

3. 외국인 의료관광에서 가장 큰 비중을 차지한 분야는 피부과였다.

Among medical tourism services used by foreigners, dermatology accounted for the largest share.

4. 지난해 외국인 의료관광 소비액 중 피부과 비중은 49%(6386억 원)로, 성형외과(28%·3649억원)를 크게 앞섰다.

Last year, dermatology made up 49% (638.6 billion won) of total foreign medical tourism spending, significantly surpassing cosmetic surgery (28%, 364.9 billion won).

5-1. 피부과 진료 중에서는 피부미용·항노화 시술(약 30만8300건)이 가장 많았으며,

Within dermatology and treatment services, skincare and anti-aging procedures (about 308,300 cases) were the most common,

followed by wrinkle improvement treatments (about 66,800 cases).

6. 성형외과에서는 쌍꺼풀 수술(약 3만7700건), 코 성형(약 5900건) 순으로 많았다.

In cosmetic surgery, double eyelid surgery (about 37,700 cases) and nose surgery (about 5,900 cases) were among the most performed.

7. 지난해 전체 부가세 환급 건수는 약 103만800건(955억원)으로, 전년 대비 169% 증가했다.

Last year, the total number of procedures across all departments reached about 1,030,800 (95.5 billion won), marking a 169% increase compared to the previous year.

8. 피부과 시술의 증가율도 눈에 띈다.

The increase in dermatological procedures is also noteworthy.

Tattoo removal surgeries increased by 589% (around 6,000 cases), skincare and anti-aging procedures by 490% (about 300,000 cases),

liposuction by 303% (around 20,000 cases), and acne treatments by 276% (about 2,000 cases), all greatly surpassing the average growth rate of 232%.
